Tying Up Loose Ends

Due to spoilers, I cannot provide details about the Final Level of Ai2U at this time.

I can, however, tease that

  • I wrote 13 different endings for the game, each narratively impactful and emotionally resonant based on player's choices within the level.
  • It has callbacks to elements, lore, and canon ending to each of the previous levels.
  • It has a high-concept, never-been-done idea that breaks the 4th wall and directly ask a question to the player.
  • I made a total of 8 new minigames for the level, 3 of which utilizes LLM in some innotive way to directly add to the gameplay.
