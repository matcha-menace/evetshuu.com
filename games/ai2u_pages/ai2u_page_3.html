Skybox

The 4th Level of Ai2U is the 1st exterior level, so we required a dynamic skybox with a day-night cycle feature to tie into the character's ability.

My skybox tool was designed to be simple but robust, and offer enough customizability for both dev and art team to add any and all features they want. The end result came out so good my Creative Director suggested me to put it up for sale on Unity Asset Store , where it currently sits at a 5/5 star rating.

Minigames

I designed, pitched, and prototyped 4 unique minigames in under a month.

Fishing

simple fishing mechanic balancing keyboard and precision mouse inputs

Stargazing

utlizing the dynamic skybox, find the stars matching the pattern

Collecting

use sound and visual cues to spot weak points on a 3D cube

Boating

using precision inputs based on rhythm to evade Siren's attacks and escape her hunt

The dev team deemed my code to be 90% shippable so they only added some architectural tweaks before shipping. Thanks guys!
