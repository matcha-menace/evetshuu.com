Skybox

The 4th Level of Ai2U is the 1st exterior level, so we required a dynamic skybox with a day-night cycle feature to tie into the character's ability.

My skybox tool was designed to be simple but robust, and offer enough customizability for both dev and art team to add any and all features they want. The end result came out so good my Creative Director suggested me to put it up for sale on Unity Asset Store, where it currently sits at a 5/5 star rating.
